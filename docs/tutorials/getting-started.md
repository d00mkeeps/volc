# Getting Started with Volc

Volc is an AI-powered fitness coach designed to help you optimize your training through intelligent guidance and seamless workout logging.

This tutorial will help you get the app on your phone so you can start training today.

## Quick Start: Download the App

The fastest way to experience Volc is to download the official app from the iOS App Store.

1. **Download**: [Volc on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/gb/app/volc/id6751469055)
2. **Onboarding**: Once installed, open the app and follow the in-app onboarding process to set your fitness goals and customize your experience.
3. **Start Training**: Ask your AI coach for a recommendation and log your first set!
